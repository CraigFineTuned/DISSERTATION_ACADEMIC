## üéØ FOUR MAJOR THEMES VALIDATED

### **THEME 1: The Great Divide**
**Definition:** Cultural schism between Service Desk (Ubuntu-aligned: collaborative, empathetic, solution-focused) and Specialist teams (ego-driven: blame-focused, knowledge-hoarding, public-shaming). Ubuntu exists but is compartmentalized rather than uniformly absent.

**Evidence Strength:** 12/14 participants (86%) - STRONG  
**Key Sub-Themes:**
- Two-culture organization (Service Desk vs Specialists)
- Historical erosion (P07: 2000 integrated ‚Üí 2025 compartmentalized)
- Translation gap ("user impact" vs "system metrics" - P12)
- Knowledge gatekeeping as power
- Boundary crossing punishment

**RQ Mapping:** RQ1 (Gap Manifestations), RQ4 (Organizational Factors)

**Defining Quotes:**
- "Ubuntu compartmentalized: Strong in Service Desk. Weak everywhere else" (P07 - 25-year veteran)
- "My team focuses on solving; they focus on 'whose fault'" (P02 - Tactical)
- "Some have all the rungs, some told to climb barefoot" (P14 - Junior)

**Evidence Strength:** 11/14 participants (79%) - STRONG  
**Key Sub-Themes:**

- Historical evidence from P07 (2000 vs 2025)

**RQ Mapping:** RQ2 (Ubuntu Bridging Potential), RQ3 (Practitioner Perceptions)
